### Project Overview & Vision

**Project Name:** Bitnal  
**Company:** Bitnal  

**Core Purpose:**  
Bitnal is a **marketing and business intelligence platform** for small sports venues (starting with archery, gun, and bowling ranges). It helps owners understand their customers deeply and make better decisions to grow their business.

**Long-term Strategy:**
- Phase 1: Build and prove on Ambrose Archery
- Phase 2: Sell to similar venues
- Phase 3: Expand booking engine to other industries while keeping the same analytics & marketing core

**The real product is the analytics + marketing intelligence.** The booking system is important but secondary.

**Core Technical Philosophy:**
- Public site must be extremely fast (< 1 second load time)
- Use single sacred HTML + external CSS themes (Zen Garden style) to enforce speed and easy visual testing
- Prioritize workflow excellence before compromising structure
- Build rich data foundation from day one
